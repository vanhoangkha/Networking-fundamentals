# 52. LAN ARCHITECTURES - You have studied various Mạng technologies: Định tuyến, Chuyển mạch, STP, EtherChannel, OSPF, FHRPs, Switch Bảo mật FEATURES, etc. - Now, let’s look tại some BASIC Mạng DESIGN / Kiến trúc - There là standard “Thực hành tốt nhất” for Mạng DESIGN - However there là một few UNIVERSAL “CORRECT ANSWERS” - The answer đến MOST general questions about Mạng DESIGN is “IT DEPENDS” - In the early stages của your NETWORKING career, you probably won’t be asked đến DESIGN NETWORKS yourself - However, to understand the NETWORKS you sẽ be CONFIGURING và Khắc phục sự cố, it’s important đến know some BASICS của Mạng DESIGN --- COMMON TERMINOLOGIES - STAR - When several DEVICES all connect đến ONE CENTRAL Thiết bị, we có thể draw them trong a “STAR” shape like below, so this là often called a “STAR Cấu trúc mạng” ![image](https://github.com/psaumur/CCNA/assets/106411237/8aeb545d-3cc0-44bf-a01e-b7e5d47deaf2) - FULL MESH - When each Thiết bị là connected đến each OTHER Thiết bị ![image](https://github.com/psaumur/CCNA/assets/106411237/cb2d12af-cf17-4ffe-a637-148014d20753) - PARTIAL MESH - When SOME DEVICES là connected đến each other but not ALL ![image](https://github.com/psaumur/CCNA/assets/106411237/01ed7fe5-317b-45c7-8baa-0cc74e502433) --- 2-TIER và 3-TIER LAN Kiến trúc - The TWO-TIER LAN DESIGN consists của TWO Hierarchical Layers: - Truy cập LAYER - DISTRIBUTION LAYER - Also called a “COLLAPSED CORE” DESIGN because it omits một layer that là found trong the THREE TIER DESIGN : THE CORE LAYER - Truy cập LAYER - The LAYER that END HOSTS connect to (PCs, Printers, Cameras, etc) - Typically, Truy cập LAYER SWITCHES have lots của PORTS cho END HOSTS đến connect to - QoS Đánh dấu là typically done here - Bảo mật Services like Cổng Bảo mật, DAI, etc là typically performed here - SWITCHPORTS có thể be PoE-Enabled cho Không dây APs, IP Phones, etc. - DISTRIBUTION LAYER - Aggregates connections từ the Truy cập LAYER SWITCHES - Typically là the border between LAYER 2 và LAYER 3 - Connects đến services such as Internet, WAN, etc - Sometimes called AGGREGATION LAYER ![image](https://github.com/psaumur/CCNA/assets/106411237/4592f4d8-5550-4428-923c-c805d2ca476f) ![image](https://github.com/psaumur/CCNA/assets/106411237/4fa26aec-536a-4ad8-8f39-e94dacc4cb3c) ![image](https://github.com/psaumur/CCNA/assets/106411237/72018e4f-113e-4921-8dc4-05079c590ee1) ![image](https://github.com/psaumur/CCNA/assets/106411237/c8326214-80e0-4702-a1c3-6dd2fbafb6e9) --- THREE-TIER Campus LAN DESIGN - In large NETWORKS với many DISTRIBUTION LAYER SWITCHES (Ví dụ trong separate buildings), the number của connections required between DISTRIBUTION LAYER SWITCHES grows rapidly ![image](https://github.com/psaumur/CCNA/assets/106411237/8b94c8e9-813b-40e0-bcd1-b27d73da31e8) - To help SCALE large LAN NETWORKS, you có thể add một CORE LAYER. ** Cisco recommends adding một CORE LAYER if there là more than THREE DISTRIBUTION LAYERS trong một single location ![image](https://github.com/psaumur/CCNA/assets/106411237/d5c1a677-38ff-425f-b91a-65a8fa37c377) - The THREE-TIER LAN DESIGN consists của THREE HIERARCHICAL LAYERS: - Truy cập LAYER - DISTRIBUTION LAYER - CORE LAYER - CORE LAYER: - Connects DISTRIBUTION LAYERS together trong large LAN NETWORKS - The focus là SPEED (”FAST TRANSPORT”) - CPU-INTENSIVE OPERATIONS, such as Bảo mật, QoS Markings / Phân loại, etc. should be avoided tại this LAYER - Connections là all LAYER 3. NO SPANNING-TREE! - Should maintain connectivity throughout the LAN even if DEVICES FAIL ![image](https://github.com/psaumur/CCNA/assets/106411237/633cee0a-8952-4b27-91a3-8653bb8e353c) --- SPINE-LEAF Kiến trúc (DATA CENTER) - CISCO ACI Kiến trúc (Application Centric Hạ tầng) uses this Kiến trúc - DATA CENTERS là dedicated spaces / buildings used đến STORE COMPUTER SYSTEMS such as SERVERS và Mạng DEVICES - Traditional DATA CENTER designs used một THREE-TIER Kiến trúc (Truy cập-DISTRIBUTION-CORE) like we just covered - This worked well when most TRAFFIC trong the DATA CENTER was NORTH-SOUTH ![image](https://github.com/psaumur/CCNA/assets/106411237/7e2ff784-d16f-4606-a186-c73223bf5582) - With the precedence của VIRTUAL SERVERS, applications là often deployed trong một DISTRIBUTED manner (across multiple physical SERVERS) which increases the amount của EAST-WEST TRAFFIC trong the DATA CENTER - The traditional THREE-TIER Kiến trúc led đến bottlenecks trong the BANDWIDTH as well as VARIABILITY trong the SERVER-TO-SERVER latency depending trên the PATH the TRAFFIC takes - To SOLVE this, SPINE-LEAF Kiến trúc (also called CLOS Kiến trúc) has become prominent trong DATA CENTERS RULES cho SPINE-LEAF Kiến trúc - Every LEAF Switch là connected đến every SPINE Switch - Every SPINE Switch là connected đến every LEAF Switch - LEAF SWITCHES do NOT connect đến other LEAF SWITCHES - SPINE SWITCHES do NOT connect đến other SPINE SWITCHES - END HOSTS (Servers, etc) ONLY connect đến LEAF SWITCHES ![image](https://github.com/psaumur/CCNA/assets/106411237/73cbe190-f589-4307-8ce4-e3de8af2f1d5) - The PATH taken bởi TRAFFIC là randomly chosen đến balance the TRAFFIC LOAD among the SPINE SWITCHES - Each SERVER là separated bởi the same number of “HOPS” (except those connected đến the same LEAF) providing CONSISTENT LATENCY cho EAST-WEST TRAFFIC --- SOHO (SMALL OFFICE / HOME OFFICE) - SMALL OFFICE / HOME OFFICE (SOHO) refers đến the office của một small company, or một small home office với few DEVICES - Doesn’t have đến be một actual home “office”; if your home has một Mạng connected đến the Internet it là considered một SOHO Mạng - SOHO NETWORKS don’t have complex needs, so all NETWORKING functions là typically provided bởi một SINGLE Thiết bị, often called a “HOME Router” or “Không dây Router” - The one Thiết bị có thể serve as a: - Router - Switch - Tường lửa - Không dây Điểm truy cập - MODEM ![image](https://github.com/psaumur/CCNA/assets/106411237/c9edf179-f333-4fec-9e95-ee291b5eb84c) ![image](https://github.com/psaumur/CCNA/assets/106411237/7d606552-8939-41c1-ad85-13face1d27f5)
